About Me

I am acreative, artistic and ambitious Film and Video Designer with a talent for coming up with innovative ideas and video designs. With a keen eye for detail and a strong understanding of various film and video design techniques, I am confident in my ability to bring any project to life.

In addition to my design skills, I am highly motivated and always strive to continue learning and growing in my field. My strong communication and collaboration skills have allowed me to effectively work with clients and team members to bring projects from concept to completion. Whether it's a short promotional video or a feature-length film, I am dedicated to delivering the highest quality work.

I graduated with honors from La Plata University in La Plata city, Argentina with a degree in the field and have since gained valuable experience working in the video editing industry for the past 5 years. My strong work ethic and commitment to providing excellent service has allowed me to make a positive impact in the lives and businesses of my clients.

In addition to my technical skills, I possess strong interpersonal and communication skills, which allow me to effectively understand and meet the needs of my clients. I pride myself in being a problem solver and finding innovative solutions to ensure the success of each project. My eagerness to learn and openness to new ideas allows me to continuously grow and improve in my field. Whether it's a small video project or a large-scale film production, I am fully committed to delivering the highest quality work and exceeding my clients' expectations.

FrontEnd Developer
